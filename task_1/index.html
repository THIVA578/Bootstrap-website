Our Beginning

HE Easa Saleh A1 Gurg, KCVO, CBE has been involved with charitable work for many decades. As the number of charitable projects that he was involved in, began to multiply, His Excellency felt it was prudent to formalize these activities and create an organization that would operate on his behalf. The creation of Easa Saleh A1 Gurg Charity Foundation was unanimously seconded by the members of the A1 Gurg family during a meeting in 2010.
